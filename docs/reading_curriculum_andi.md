# Reading Curriculum for Andi (K-2)

## Scope & Sequence

### Level 1: Phonemic Awareness (Pre-K - Early K)
Skills to master before moving to Level 2:
- [ ] Hearing and identifying rhyming words
- [ ] Counting syllables in words
- [ ] Identifying beginning sounds
- [ ] Identifying ending sounds
- [ ] Blending sounds into words (c-a-t â†’ cat)

### Level 2: Letter Recognition & Formation (Early K)
Skills to master before moving to Level 3:
- [ ] Recognizing all 26 uppercase letters
- [ ] Recognizing all 26 lowercase letters
- [ ] Writing all 26 letters (uppercase and lowercase)
- [ ] Knowing the sounds each letter makes
- [ ] Distinguishing letters from numbers and words

### Level 3: Phonics & Decoding (Mid K - Early 1st)
Skills to master before moving to Level 4:
- [ ] Blending consonant-vowel-consonant (CVC) words
- [ ] Reading short vowel words (cat, dog, map, pin, sun)
- [ ] Identifying vowel sounds (short vowels)
- [ ] Reading sight words (high-frequency words)
- [ ] Segmenting words into sounds

### Level 4: Fluency & Accuracy (1st Grade)
Skills to master before moving to Level 5:
- [ ] Reading CVC words fluently (80+ words per minute)
- [ ] Reading CVCe words (cake, bike, home)
- [ ] Reading digraphs (sh, ch, th, wh, ph)
- [ ] Reading blends (bl, cl, fl, br, cr, dr)
- [ ] Reading 100+ high-frequency sight words

### Level 5: Comprehension Strategies (1st - 2nd Grade)
Skills to master before moving to Level 6:
- [ ] Asking questions before, during, and after reading
- [ ] Making predictions about stories
- [ ] Identifying main idea and key details
- [ ] Retelling a story in order (beginning, middle, end)
- [ ] Making connections (text-to-self, text-to-world)

### Level 6: Advanced Phonics & Vocabulary (2nd Grade)
Skills to master:
- [ ] Long vowel patterns (ai, ay, ea, ee, oa, ow)
- [ ] R-controlled vowels (ar, or, er, ir, ur)
- [ ] Diphthongs (oi, oy, ou, ow)
- [ ] Prefixes and suffixes (-un, -re, -ed, -ing, -s, -es)
- [ ] Compound words (sun + flower = sunflower)
- [ ] Context clues for unknown words

---

## Implementation Notes

### Prerequisites
- Create new topic: "Reading (K-2)"
- Each level = 1 curriculum node
- Questions tagged by level for adaptive learning

### Question Types by Level
- **Levels 1-2**: Audio-assisted letter/sound identification (future feature)
- **Levels 3-4**: Word reading, sound identification, word matching
- **Levels 5-6**: Passage reading, comprehension questions, vocabulary

### Mastery Thresholds
- Levels 1-2: 80% mastery (foundational, need solid base)
- Levels 3-4: 75% mastery
- Levels 5-6: 70% mastery (comprehension more nuanced)
